About us

Why Uncommonsense?

We have spent years advocating what we see as the right approach and to us it is not rocket science, but common sense. Although not alone in our mission, we continue to be surprised by how many people just don't get it and we want to help. A lot of our work has been with large financial institutions but we have experience across most sectors and also with small start-ups. We want to expand our work into smaller, more local businesses around South West London and hope to partner to make these businesses a success.

Who we are

We are a close network of friends and we have all worked together. We have spent our careers advising and changing organisations and delivering complex solutions in challenging environments. We know how hard it can be to get the help you need from the consulting market and want to offer an equitable and transparent model that works for all parties. We are passionate about community and culture, within Uncommonsense and when working with our clients.

What we do

We have a broad offering based on our members, we are well equipped to help with most of the challenges facing banks and other financial sector organisations but we believe these skills and experience can be effective in any organisation. We can deliver products/value, help you to transform your organisations from bottom to top with lean/agile approaches, coach and help to change the leadership/organisational model and much much more. We won't do it to you but we are there to help you achieve your true business goals. We are full of ideas so will be launching some interesting products.

Our philosophy

Our philosophy is to only work on topics we find interesting and on challenges where we can add value. We won't try and help where we are not the right support and will happily point you in the right direction when this is the case. We want to share and embed the uncommonsense energy and fun wherever we go, come on life is too short so we should all love what we do!
