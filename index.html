About us

Why Uncommonsense?

We have spent many years advocating what we see as the right approach and to us it is not rocket science, but common sense. Although not alone in our mission, we continue to be surprised by how many people don't get it. We don't always work in the transformation space and can also help with your other challenges, for example, we have a breadth of RegTech experience. We are also passionate about product development, which can be for our clients or our internal projects. We have a vision for equitable product development so individual contributions are understood and recognised.

Who we are

We are a close network of friends and we have all worked together. We have spent our careers advising and changing organisations and delivering complex solutions in challenging environments. We know how hard it can be to get the help you need from the consulting market and want to offer an equitable and transparent model that works for all parties. We are passionate about community and culture, within Uncommonsense and when working for our clients.

What we do

We have a broad offering based on our members, we are well equipped to help with most of the challenges facing banks and other financial sector organisations. Where transferable, we are also happy to support clients in other sectors. We can deliver software, advise on the latest regulations and help you to transform your organisations from bottom to top with lean/agile approaches, empowerment of teams, changing the leadership model, focusing on skills and development (especially non-technical skills and behaviours) and ultimately building on intrinsic motivation. We want to help others find the right approach for their organisations and you probably don't want to engage us is you are not wanting to change. We are full of ideas so will be launching some interesting products.

Our philosophy

Our philosophy is to only work on topics we find interesting and on challenges where we can add value. We won't try and help where we are not the right support and will happily point you in the right direction when this is the case. We want to share and embed the uncommonsense energy and fun wherever we go, come on life is too short so we should love what we do!
